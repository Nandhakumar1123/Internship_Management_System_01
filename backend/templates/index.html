About the System

Our Internship Management System simplifies the process of applying, posting, and managing internships. Students can register, browse available opportunities, and apply directly. HR professionals can post internship opportunities, shortlist, and select candidates seamlessly.

👩‍🎓 For Students

  • Register and create your profile
  • Browse and apply for internships
  • Track your application status

👔 For HR

  • Post new internship opportunities
  • View and shortlist student applications
  • Select and communicate with candidates
